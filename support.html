How can we help you ?

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get connected?

    To get connected, I will send a KYC form to you. Kindly fill the form accurately indicating your preferred plan and attached a valid means of identification (residential) or CAC registration form (corporate) and revert (send) to me. Upon receive of the form; you will receive a proforma invoice from our account department.

    Kindly make payment into the company’s account detailed found on the invoice and send a proof of payment to the account department and your connection process will commence, this process include our customer support representative contacting you requesting access for an engineer to come over for splicing, wiring and installation of the fiber cable, once that is done our technical engineer will come over for configuration of your devices and have your internet up and running.

    This process will take 10/5 working days (that depend on the location). Note that you will be informed before an engineer comes to your location and quality of the service will be confirmed.

  • How much data is allowed?

    Our internet service is a priority; we offer you an unlimited internet, there are no limit to your data consumption or download using fiber optic internet technology.

  • Is there a trial period?

    We would love to grant that but due to the fact that our service is a wired service we can’t give the pleasure of that, notwithstanding; we absolutely guarantee you of total satisfaction.

  • Do you have service in my area?

    For us to ascertain this I will need a detailed location address, this is needed to carry out a survey remotely.

  • How many days will this connection process last?

    Once payment is confirmed, the internet will be up in 10 or 5 working days (depend on the location of your lead)

  • What are the devices provided?

    A fiber modern (ONU) and a Access Point (Router)

  • What is the minimum speed I may likely get in a shared plan?

    For a shared internet plan, you may likely get 3mbps minimum speed.

  • Do I get notification on when monthly payment is due?

    Yes, you will. We offer a prepaid service and at the third week of a present subscribed month you will receive an invoice form our account department containing your subscription payment detail for the next month.

  • What are MRC and NRC?

    MRC (monthly recurring charge), this is the payment for the desired plan and this payment is done monthly. You can make decision on how you want your billing to be sent i.e. monthly, quarterly, yearly and so on.

    NRC (non-recurring charge), this is basically your one time paid connection fee or set-up cost

  • Is this service unlimited?

    Yes, it is a truly unlimited service, laying a fiber cable from point A to point B, no data cap.

  • I do lot of gaming, skyping, and streaming of videos, what is the required plan that can work for this?

    For a residential plan we have the fiber 10 having a speed up to 10mbps, Absolutely you can do all this but not at the same time as all these activities consumes lot of speed and this might affect your internet.

    For a corporate plan, you are getting a dedicated internet and that means that the speed you subscribe for is what you are getting, notwithstanding, you have to get the required speed to carry out activities of such? We may request to know what your current bandwidth you are using, what activities is currently done on it, how many users and we can suggest a suitable plan, if you don’t mind.

  • Is my privacy protected?

    Yes.

  • What type of router are you providing?

    Most routers will work with our service but we will provide a mikrotik router and a fiber modem.

  • What is the difference between a shared internet service and dedicated internet service?

    For a dedicated service, it is a premium service; there is a static IP allocated to the user. This service is dedicated to one user; the bandwidth is not shared among other users and devices. This service is the best for organizations as the speed is guaranteed.

    For the shared service, all bandwidth is split among all users and devices, in a shared service there is no 100% guaranteed speed.

  • What is your broadband coverage area?

    You can fill our contact form if you have any further questions. You can also send email to 247@anywair.ng or call us at +234-90-6455-555

  • Do I get incentives or discounts on advance payments?

    Yes, at TeleWeb, progressive discounts apply when you pay annually.

  • Can I make advance payments?

    We offer various payment frequencies, including monthly, quarterly, every six months, and annually.

  • Can I upgrade my plan?

    Yes, you can upgrade your plan by sending a request for update mail to our accounts department.

  • What methods of payments do you accept?

    We accept payment to our bank account payment details such as account information will be provided on your invoice.

  • How fast is the TeleWeb Broadband?

    TeleWeb offers download speeds up to 10mbps and uploads speeds up to 10mbps and above as this varies with your preferred plan.

  • Is TeleWeb Internet affected by weather?

    TeleWeb provides you uninterrupted internet access with no interference.
