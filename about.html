Who we are

Our Story

AnyWair is the Registered Trademark of TeleWeb Services Ltd, an ASP & IOT company Licensed by the Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC) to build, maintain and, operate Wireless Networks and Private Network Links (PNL), with the goal to advance businesses in Nigeria and across Africa with latest infrastructure, technology and solutions; thus, driving productivity, business agility and reliability.

We are the first IoT company in Nigeria to offer SD-WAN deployed over our CDN infrastructure. We are progressively building our network across the nation and diversifying into Artificial intelligence as the world continues to experience major technology advancements.

TeleWeb has been in operations since early 2019, with HeadQuarters in Lagos, Nigeria and with a branch office in Abuja, Nigeria.
