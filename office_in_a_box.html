Office in a Box

What is Office-in-a-Box?

Office in a Box is the latest in rapid deploy telecoms solutions. Providing internet connectivity, landlines, and wifi to any remote site in as little as 24 hours using the UK's 4G network.

What can it be used for?

Office-in-a-box is a device operating with the SD-WAN technology which provides a redundant internet service and it is portable. It provides solutions to many industries around us. Some of the industries iclude; Healthcare, Financial Industries, Malls, Estates, SMEs, Restuarants, Hotels/Motels.

How does it work?

Customize As Necessary

This is YOUR solution, so you can load any applications you need to support your business.

Break The Chains That Bind

The C3 Office-in-a-Box solution is accessible from anywhere, at any time, from almost any device. Never be tied to your desk again! The supported devices include PC, Mac, iPad, & Android.

Flexible & Scalable

Additional users, disk space, and other resources can be provisioned in just minutes! Need resources on a temporary basis? Not a problem.

Additional Options

Need even more? C3 can add Microsoft SharePoint, Visio, SQL Server, QuickBooks and other common software packages to your solution.

Future Proof

If the needs of your organization exceed the capabilities of the Office-in-a-Box solution, your users, applications, and data can easily be migrated to our Enterprise platform.

Be Prepared For The Worst

By moving your applications and data out of your office and into the Cloud, you are fully protected from disasters such as flood, fire, theft, or hurricane. No power in your office? Simply grab your laptop or tablet, find the nearest internet connection and get back to work!
